How to Download NCERT Book / CBSE Book for Class 11 Physics?

Follow the steps as listed below to download CBSE Grade 11 Physics NCERT Books PDF:

– 1st Step: Visit the official website of NCERT Books – https://ncert.nic.in/textbook.php
– 2nd Step: Move to the “Class” tab and select “Class XI” from the drop-down menu.
– 3rd Step: Click on the “Select Subject“.
– 4th Step: Choose the subject “Physics” from the drop-down menu.
– 5th Step: Now Click on the Part for which you have to download the book.
– 6th Step: Click on the link “GO“.
– 7th Step: Your NCERT Books for CBSE Class 11 Physics PDF will be downloaded.

FAQs Regarding Grade 11 Physics NCERT Books

The frequently asked questions regarding CBSE Class 11 Physics books are given below:

Q. Which book is best for 11th physics?


A. Apart from Class 11 Physics book, students can refer to the following books:
-Concepts of Physics by HC Verma.
-Understanding Physics series from Arihant publishing house.
-S.L Arora: New Simplified Physics
Q. Is NCERT enough for Class 11 Physics?
A. Yes, NCERT is more than enough for Class 11 annual exams.

Q. Which is better HC Verma or DC Pandey?


A. Both books are good. But most of the students prefer HC Verma for understanding basic concepts.
Whereas DC Pandey helps to improve the efficiency of solving problems.

Q. How many NCERT Textbooks are there for Class 11 Physics, and how many chapters each contain?1111,


A. NCERT Physics syllabus is divided into two parts:
Part 1 and Part 2. There are a total of 15 chapters;
Part 1 consists of 8 chapters and the remaining 7 chapters are there in part 2. The chapters in Physics Part-1 are '
Chapter 1:Physical World,
Chapter 2: Units and Measurements,
Chapter 3: Motion in a Straight Line,
Chapter 4: Motion in a Plane,
Chapter 5: Laws of Motion,
Chapter 6: Work, Energy, and Power,
Chapter 7: Systems of Particles and Rotational Motion,
Chapter 8: Gravitation. The chapters in Part 2 are
Chapter 9: Mechanical Properties of Solids,
Chapter 10: Mechanical Properties of Fluids,
Chapter 11: Thermal Properties of Matter,
Chapter 12: Thermodynamics,
Chapter 13: Kinetic Theory, Chapter 14: Oscillations, Chapter 15: Waves.
